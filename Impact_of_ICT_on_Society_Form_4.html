Impact of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) on Society

Computer Studies Form 4 Notes

Employment Automation Health Environment Culture Artificial Intelligence

Effects on Employment

  • Job creation (Programmers, IT managers, System analysts)
  • Job replacement (Manual tasks automated)
  • Job displacement (Transfer of workers)

Effects on Automated Production

Advantages

  • Increased efficiency
  • Improved customer service
  • Reduced accidents

Disadvantages

  • High initial cost
  • Unemployment in labour-intensive areas
  • Resistance to change

Workers' Health Issues

  • Repetitive Strain Injuries (RSI)
  • Eye strain and headaches
  • Stress and isolation
  • Radiation and toxic exposure

Environmental Issues

  • High energy consumption
  • Electronic waste disposal
  • Toxic battery materials

Cultural Effects

  • Moral challenges
  • Computer crimes (hacking, piracy, fraud)

Artificial Intelligence & Future Trends

  • Expert systems
  • Natural language processing
  • Voice recognition & synthesis
  • Robotics
  • Expanded information superhighway
